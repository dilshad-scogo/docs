## What is SIA?

SIA is an intelligent AI assistant platform designed to revolutionize technical support operations. It handles pre-sale, post-sale, and after-sale support through advanced AI capabilities, automatically learning from your website content and knowledge base to provide accurate, consistent support.

## How It Works

### Quick Setup Process

1. Sign up at [scogo.ai](https://scogo.ai)
2. Enter your website URL
3. SIA automatically creates an AI agent
4. Embed the provided JS snippet
5. Customize workflows and knowledge base

<Note>
Your AI agent automatically starts learning from your website content as soon as it's created.
</Note>
