## Overview

Create your SIA workspace and start building AI assistants in minutes.

### Account Types
- Individual Account
- Team Account
- Enterprise Solution

## Creating Your Account

### Quick Start
1. Visit [sia.scogo.ai/signup](https://sia.scogo.ai/signup)
2. Enter required information:
   - Business email
   - Company name
   - Password
3. Select your account type
4. Verify your email
5. Complete organization setup

<Tip>
Use your business email to automatically connect with your organization's SSO.
</Tip>

## Organization Setup

### Initial Configuration
1. Set organization name
2. Choose industry type
3. Define workspace preferences
4. Invite team members

### Team Invitations
Invite colleagues:
1. Go to "Team Management"
2. Click "Invite Members"
3. Enter email addresses
4. Set role permissions

## Account Verification

### Email Verification
1. Check your inbox
2. Click verification link
3. Complete profile setup

### Domain Verification
For enterprise accounts:
1. Add DNS records
2. Verify domain ownership
3. Enable SSO integration

## Getting Started
