    ## Create Your Account
    
    Follow these steps to create your Scogo.ai account:

    1. Visit [Scogo.ai](https://scogo.ai)
    2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
    3. Fill in your details:
       - Email address
       - Password (minimum 8 characters)
       - Full name
    4. Click "Create Account"
    5. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox

    <Tip>
    Use a strong password with letters, numbers, and special characters for better security.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Login">
    ## Access Your Account
    
    To log in to your existing account:

    1. Go to [Scogo.ai](https://scogo.ai)
    2. Click "Login" in the top right corner
    3. Enter your credentials:
       - Email address
       - Password
    4. Click "Log In"

    ### Password Reset
    1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Check your inbox for reset instructions
    4. Create a new password

    <Note>
    If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password?" to reset it through your email.
    </Note>
  </Tab>

## Account Management

### Account Settings
After logging in, you can:
- Update your profile information
- Change your password
- Manage email preferences
- Set up two-factor authentication

## Creating Your First Agent

Once logged in, create your first AI agent:

### Step 1: Access Agent Creation
1. Navigate to your dashboard
2. Click "Create New Agent"
3. Choose a template or start from scratch

### Step 2: Configure Your Agent

| Setting | Description |
|---------|------------|
| Agent Name | Give your agent a unique identifier |
| Purpose | Define what tasks your agent will perform |
| Language | Select primary language for interaction |
| Knowledge Base | Upload or link relevant documents |

### Step 3: Train Your Agent
1. Provide training data or examples
2. Set up response parameters
3. Define conversation flow
4. Test your agent's responses

<Warning>
Always test your agent thoroughly before deploying it to production.
</Warning>

## Security Recommendations

- Use a unique, strong password
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Never share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared devices
- Regularly review your account activity

## Managing Your Agent

After creation, you can:
- Monitor agent performance
- Update training data
- Adjust response parameters
- View conversation logs
- Export analytics

### Best Practices
- Keep your training data organized and relevant
- Regularly update your agent's knowledge base
- Test your agent in different scenarios
- Monitor user interactions for improvement opportunities
