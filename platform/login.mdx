## Overview

Learn how to securely access your SIA workspace and manage your authentication settings.

### Authentication Methods

SIA supports multiple authentication methods:
- Email and Password
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Multi-factor Authentication (MFA)

## Logging In

### Standard Login
1. Visit [sia.scogo.ai](https://sia.scogo.ai)
2. Enter your email address
3. Provide your password
4. Click "Sign In"

<Note>
Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include numbers and special characters.
</Note>

### SSO Login
1. Click "Continue with SSO"
2. Enter your organization domain
3. Complete authentication through your identity provider

## Security Features

### Multi-factor Authentication
Enable MFA for enhanced security:
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select "Security"
3. Enable "Two-factor Authentication"
4. Follow the setup wizard

### Session Management
- Auto-logout after 24 hours of inactivity
- Concurrent session limitations
- IP-based access controls

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Forgot Password">
    1. Click "Forgot Password" on login screen
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Check your inbox for reset instructions
    4. Create new password within 24 hours
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Account Locked">
    If your account is locked:
    1. Wait 30 minutes
    2. Try logging in again
    3. Contact support if issues persist
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
