## Introduction

SIA's AI agents are designed to provide intelligent responses by leveraging your website content, knowledge base, and FAQ documentation. This guide explains how our AI processes information and interacts with users through various channels.

## Handling Queries

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/sia-logo-light.png" alt="Query Processing Flow" />
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="brain" title="Query Processing">
    How SIA processes user queries:
    1. Receives user input
    2. Searches through available knowledge sources:
       - Scraped website content
       - Uploaded documentation
       - FAQ database
    3. Generates contextual responses
    
    <Tip>
      The more comprehensive your knowledge base, the more accurate the responses will be.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="circle-exclamation" title="Fallback Responses">
    When information isn't found:
    1. Agent acknowledges knowledge gap
    2. Provides alternative suggestions
    3. Offers to:
       - Connect with human support
       - Submit query for future training
       - Rephrase the question

    <Warning>
      Configure fallback responses in your agent settings to ensure appropriate handling of unknown queries.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
